About Our Bakery

Welcome to Eunice's Back Rd Bakery, where every cookie tells a story of passion, quality, and tradition. Handcrafted in small batches using only the finest ingredients, our gourmet cookies are baked with love right here in Huntsville, Texas.

Each oversized cookie is a masterpiece - soft inside, perfectly crispy on the edges, and absolutely loaded with premium ingredients. We believe in doing one thing exceptionally well: making cookies that are as generous in size as they are in flavor.

From our family to your table, taste the difference that genuine care and premium ingredients make. Order today and experience Big Back Gourmet Cookies!
